Most DTC brands are leaving money on the table. Not because their product is bad, but because they have blind spots across their growth engine. This checklist covers the six core levers that drive sustainable, profitable growth. Score yourself honestly, and you will know exactly where to focus your next sprint.

Lever 01 of 06
Offer
0 / 8
  • Clear value proposition in under 10 words
  • Pricing anchored against a higher reference point
  • At least one bundle offer live on site
  • Subscription or replenishment option (if applicable)
  • Free shipping threshold set above AOV
  • Gift with purchase or bonus offer
  • Money-back guarantee prominently displayed
  • Upsell and cross-sell on product and cart pages
📊
Lever 02 of 06
CRO
0 / 10
  • Site loads in under 3 seconds on mobile
  • Mobile conversion rate within 30% of desktop
  • Hero section communicates the offer in 5 seconds
  • Trust badges above the fold
  • Product pages have reviews (minimum 10 per product)
  • Add-to-cart button visible without scrolling
  • Cart page shows order summary and savings
  • Checkout is 3 steps or fewer
  • Exit-intent popup captures abandoning visitors
  • Site search is functional and returns relevant results
✉️
Lever 03 of 06
Email
0 / 10
  • Welcome flow live (5+ emails)
  • Abandoned cart flow live (3+ emails)
  • Browse abandonment flow live (3+ emails)
  • Post-purchase flow live (4+ emails)
  • Win-back flow live (3+ emails for 60+ day lapsed)
  • Campaign calendar planned monthly
  • List segmented by purchase behaviour (F0/F1+)
  • Email revenue is 25%+ of total
  • Deliverability rate above 95%
  • Unsubscribe rate under 0.3% per campaign
✍️
Lever 04 of 06
Content
0 / 8
  • Blog published at least 2x per month
  • Social media posting at least 3x per week
  • UGC collection system in place
  • Content repurposed across 3+ platforms
  • SEO keyword targets defined for top 20 pages
  • Content calendar planned 30 days ahead
  • Brand story documented and used consistently
  • Customer FAQ content published on site
🚀
Lever 05 of 06
Ads
0 / 8
  • Meta Ads ROAS above 3x
  • Google Ads ROAS above 4x
  • Creative refreshed every 2 to 3 weeks
  • TOF/MOF/BOF funnel structure in place
  • Retargeting audiences configured (site visitors, cart abandoners, purchasers)
  • Ad spend under 30% of revenue
  • At least 3 ad creative angles tested per month
  • Lookalike audiences built from purchaser data
🤝
Lever 06 of 06
Outreach
0 / 6
  • Influencer or creator partnership programme active
  • Affiliate or referral programme live
  • PR or press mentions in last 90 days
  • Wholesale or retail partnerships explored
  • Strategic brand collaborations identified
  • Customer referral programme with incentive
